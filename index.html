Student Budget Chilli

Ingredients:

  • Onion — 1-2 yellow or white chopped corse
  • Bell pepper — 1-2 chopped corse
  • Canned tomatoes — 30oz
  • Canned refried beans — 30oz
  • Chilli powder — 3-4 tablespoons
  • Salt — to personal taste
  • Ground Cumin — 2-3 tablespoons
  • Paprika — 1-2 tablespoons
  • Ground meat — 1-2 pounds
  • Water — At least 4-5 cups

How to make:

  1. Cook meat on medium heated pan with a teaspon of oil till thoroughly cooked.
  2. Add all ingredients to a large pot and place on low heat.
  3. Cover and let simmer for at least 1-2 hours and stir occasionally.
